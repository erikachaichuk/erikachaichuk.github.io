This part is dedicated to a brief discussion and visualisation on the topic of "How can we build a sustainable planet",
which was a presented during the Economics Observatory festoval, 2025.


The panel of experts discussed the ways of combining the economic growth with sustainability, which is crucial
taking into consideration the current environmental problems the societies face.


It was mentioned on the conference talk that CO2 greenhouse emission is a complex challenge, being both significant
environmental threat and economic driver. CO2 is the primary greenhouse gas, it traps heat in the atmosphere, leading
to a global warming.


At the same tiume, CO2 emissions are often associated with an economic growth. CO2 emissions are largely tied to industrial
processes and energy production, which are key drivers of economic expansion.


Building a sustainable planet through the reduction of CO2 greenhouse gas emissions requires a multi-faceted approach
